Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 2026

TL;DR

Grain does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Everything stays on your device.

Data we collect

Grain collects no personal information. We do not ask for your name, email address, or any form of identification.

All habit data — including habit names, tap records, and streaks — is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage and is never transmitted to any server.

Data storage

  • All data is stored locally on your device only
  • No cloud sync or remote backup in v1
  • Deleting the app removes all associated data
  • We have no access to any data on your device

Notifications

If you enable reminders, Grain uses your device's local notification system. Notification preferences are stored locally and are never sent to external servers.

Subscriptions

Grain offers an optional Pro subscription managed through Apple's App Store In-App Purchase system. Billing and payment information is handled entirely by Apple and RevenueCat. We do not receive or store your payment details.

For Apple's privacy practices, see apple.com/legal/privacy.

Third-party services

  • RevenueCat — subscription management (see their privacy policy)
  • Apple App Store — billing and distribution

No analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs are included in the app.

Children's privacy

Grain does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Since no data is collected at all, this app is safe for all ages.

Changes to this policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will revise the effective date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
